Rank My Major

Hello! Welcome to Rank My Major! There is a lot of stress and confusion when it comes to picking a major. I hope that with the responses to the survey below, we can all make the process easier for those who are still trying to decide!

The Pillars

The pillars that we will focus on in order to rank majors are:

  • Percent of graduates that got jobs in their field of study?
  • What was the difficulty of the major?
  • Would you recommend that others pursue your degree?
